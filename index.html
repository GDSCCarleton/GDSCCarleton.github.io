Google Developer Student Club

@ Carleton University

Developer Student Club (DSC) is a Google Developers program for university students to learn web, mobile, and google technologies in a peer-to-peer learning environment.

WHAT WE DO

The Carleton Google Developers student club provides access to numerous workshops, information sessions and events, student-community collaborative projects and a community committed to growing in all areas of tech. Open for all majors and years!
